About Me

I am a Postdoc (Yuxiu Young Scholar) in the School of Artificial Intelligence at Nanjing University. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computational Mathematics from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2013. In 2019, I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Software and Theory from the Institution of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Jian Zhang. From 2019 to 2021, I joined the FMV group, Johannes Kepler Universität Linz, Austria, and worked with Prof. Armin Biere, as a postdoctoral researcher. In 2021, I was selected by Yuxiu Young Scholars Program of Nanjing University, and then joined the School of Artificial Intelligence.

Research Interests

  • Automated Reasoning:
    Solving satisfiability problems and solution counting problems on propositional logic formulas or first-order logic formulas. Specifically, computing the volume of high-dimensional convex polytopes or counting the number of lattice points inside.
  • Abductive Learning:
    How to bridge the gap between learning and reasoning? Abductive learning (ABL) is an answer to this question. We have an ABL research group with Prof. Wang-Zhou Dai, and advised by Prof. Zhi-hua Zhou.

致2024年南京大学人工智能学院夏令营营员: 欢迎对自动推理约束求解、反绎学习 (ABL) 感兴趣的，且有志于从事科研工作的营员报考我的研究生。可与我通过邮件联系，并附上个人简历以及研究兴趣说明。

For prospective students: We are currently working on some exiting topics, such as, counting solutions of linear inequalities, reducing the knowledge constraints in ABL, and obtaining explainable and trustworthy AI models by ABL. If you are self-motivated and passionate about these research topics, feel free to contact me via email.
